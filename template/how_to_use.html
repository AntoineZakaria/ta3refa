How To Use



Welcome To Ta3refa ,Here you can buy/sell any product with numerous options

  1. First you neeed to register as seller or customer by clicking the register button top left
  2. Then you neeed to fill your info in the seller or customer form ,then click register
  3. Now check your E-mail ,you should recieve a message from us. if you got it click the button to redirect you again to tarefa
  4. The only thing you need now is to click the login button and type your username and password

Now you are logged in what to do next

  1. You can filter all the products in your home page by star rating or by price range
  2. You can see any product details by clicking on it
  3. After some browsing you may need click on the offers&suggestions button on the left in th home page
    4. You will find suggestions&offers that you will certainly like
  4. Now that you like a product ,click Add to Cart Button
    5. Now you will notice the cart value is 1 now instead of zero



  5. Finally, click the Cart button ,and choose your desired shipping and payment oprions And click Complete purchase



  6. After the system checks your credentials ,an E-mail will be sent to you including your digital reset with shipping VAT (if you chose Express shipping)



  7. Did you get the product ? Share your thoughts and rating in the product Reviews section

Are You registered as a seller??

Notice: You can do everything mentioned before as a customer

  1. After Logging in ,A button in the navbar called dashboard will appear
  2. Once you click it you can see your balance ,products ,and if you scroll down you can see the ADD product form
  3. after you click Add product your product will be shown on your dashboard ,you can edit or delete your product from your dashboard
    4. Please keep an eye on your current balance, your products stock ,and of course your customers reviews
